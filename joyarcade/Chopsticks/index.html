Game Rules

  • Start: you and AI both begin with (1,1).
  • Attack: target += attacker; if target is 5 or more, it becomes 0.
  • Dead hands (0) cannot attack or be attacked.
  • Merge: only if both hands are alive and total is 4 or less.
  • Split: choose two values from 0-4 that keep same total.
  • You win when AI has (0,0). You lose when you have (0,0).
  • AI difficulty levels adjust how many moves ahead it thinks.
  • Have fun and try to outsmart the AI!
